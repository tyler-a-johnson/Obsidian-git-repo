# Fermi Estimation
- The "How much toilet paper is used in a year"  is an example of an Estimation Problem
- Makes estimates of the order of magnitude of a result
- A good technique for verifying a claim.

```python

pop = 25e6
on_welfare = 0.1 * pop
amount = 10000

total = amount * on_welfare

fraud = 2e9
fraud / total

```
Out: 0.08
